DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
Maharashtra Candidate

- SSC Mark list.
- Transfer / Leaving Certificate of X std.
- Gap Certificate (if any).
- Caste Certificate (for Reserved Category).
- Caste Validity Certificate (for Reserved Category).
- Aadhar card.
- Passport size photograph-3 copies.

Out of Maharashtra Candidate

- SSC Mark list.
- HSC Mark list.
- Transfer / Leaving Certificate of X std.
- Gap Certificate (if any).
- Migration Certificate.
- Passport size photograph-3 copies.
- State/District Change Certificate
